Presence Check Software

Presence Check is attendance tracking and management software.

Presence Check is a software solution for presence and time tracking and management for companies, organizations, events.

This software print badge with barcode, register access with barcode scanner on access points or remote locations with geolocation, and automate the process of presence and time management.

  • Price: 500 EUR
Features
  • One centralized website with data
  • Unlimited local and remote access points
  • Unlimited local and remote users
  • Unrestricted geographic area
  • Automatic time and hours calculation
  • Reports with time, geolocation, ipaddress
  • User authentication and management
  • Mobile ready for phones and tablets
Technologies
  • Asp.Net 4.0
  • SqlServer 2008 Express
  • Windows, IIS
